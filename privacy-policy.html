2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: phone number, name, email address (via phone OTP, Google, or Apple sign-in)
  • Profile and health data: age, gender, height, weight, body fat percentage, fitness goals, city/location
  • Nutrition data: calorie and macro goals, food diary entries, water intake, weight log
  • Food photos: images you take or upload for AI-powered calorie and nutrition analysis
  • Chat data: messages and interactions with Nova, the AI Fitness Coach
  • Workout data: workout sessions completed, exercise preferences, program enrolment and progress
  • Support requests: email address and message content when you contact us

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device data: device model, operating system version, app version, language, time zone
  • Usage data: features used, screens viewed, session duration, interaction events
  • Analytics identifiers: anonymous IDs assigned by analytics services (not tied to your real identity)
  • Push notification tokens: device tokens for delivering push notifications (only with your consent)

2.3 Information from Third-Party Integrations

  • Apple Health / Health Connect: step count, active calories burned (only with your explicit permission; we read but do not write to these services)
  • Subscription data: purchase status, subscription plan, expiration date (via our subscription management provider; we do not receive payment card details)

2.4 Points and Gamification Data

  • Points balance, earning history (from food logging, step tracking, workout completion, Instagram sharing, and other qualifying activities)
  • Points redemption history

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Providing the App and its features: Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b))
  • Processing food photos via AI: Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b))
  • Providing AI Fitness Coach (Nova) responses: Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b))
  • Sending push notifications: Consent (Art. 6(1)(a))
  • Analytics and product improvement: Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f))
  • Reading Apple Health / Health Connect data: Explicit consent (Art. 6(1)(a))
  • Processing subscription payments: Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b))
  • Responding to support requests: Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f))
  • Instagram sharing for points: Consent (Art. 6(1)(a))

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide AI-powered food recognition and calorie/macro tracking
  • Calculate personalised nutrition goals, TDEE, and health scores
  • Deliver AI Fitness Coach (Nova) conversations and recommendations
  • Track and manage workout sessions, programs, and exercise history
  • Sync activity data (steps, active calories) from Apple Health or Health Connect
  • Operate the points and gamification system, including earning and redemption
  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Send push notifications (reminders, progress updates, workout schedules) — only with your consent
  • Analyse usage patterns to improve the App (aggregated, anonymised)
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

5. AI Processing

5.1 Food Photo Analysis

When you scan a food photo, the image and any accompanying text description are sent to a third-party AI service (e.g., OpenAI API or equivalent) for analysis. The AI provider processes this data solely to return nutrition results and does not use it to train their models. We do not send your name, email, or any identifying information to the AI provider — only the food image and description.

5.2 AI Fitness Coach (Nova)

When you interact with Nova, your messages and relevant context (such as your fitness goals, nutrition data, and recent activity) are sent to a third-party AI service for processing. This enables Nova to provide personalised, context-aware fitness and nutrition guidance. We do not send your name or email to the AI provider. Conversations with Nova are stored on our servers to maintain chat continuity and improve the service.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following categories of service providers, solely for the purposes described in this policy:

  • AI Service Provider (e.g., OpenAI): food photo analysis, Nova chat processing — food images, text descriptions, chat messages
  • Subscription Management Provider (e.g., Apphud or equivalent): purchase events, subscription status
  • Firebase (Google): analytics, crash reporting, push notifications — anonymous usage data, crash logs, notification delivery
  • Analytics Providers (e.g., Amplitude, AppsFlyer): anonymous usage events, install source, anonymous device ID
  • Cloud Infrastructure Provider (e.g., AWS): hosting and data storage — all app data is stored on secure cloud servers

All service providers are contractually obligated to process data only on our behalf and in accordance with this policy.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States (AI providers, analytics services, cloud infrastructure). These transfers are necessary for providing the App's functionality. Where required by GDPR, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, or the service provider's adequacy certification, to ensure appropriate safeguards.

For Malaysian users, we comply with the cross-border data transfer requirements under the PDPA.

8. Data Retention

  • Nutrition and workout data: stored on our servers and associated with your account. Deleted when you delete your account or request data erasure.
  • Food photos: processed in real-time by the AI provider. Photos from successful scans are not stored on our servers after analysis is complete. However, if you submit a missing food report, the associated food photo may be retained on our servers for the purpose of reviewing and adding the item to our nutrition database. These photos are deleted once the food item has been added or the report has been resolved.
  • Nova chat history: retained for the duration of your account to maintain conversation continuity. Deleted upon account deletion.
  • Analytics data: retained for up to 26 months, then automatically deleted.
  • Support correspondence: retained for up to 12 months after resolution.
  • Subscription records: retained as required by tax and accounting regulations (typically 3–7 years).
  • Points and gamification data: retained for the duration of your account. Deleted upon account deletion.

9. Your Rights

9.1 Rights Under GDPR (EU/EEA/UK Users)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten") — request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction — request that we limit processing of your data
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw consent at any time (for consent-based processing)
  • Lodge a complaint — file a complaint with your local data protection authority

9.2 Rights Under CCPA (California Residents)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know — request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we collected
  • Delete — request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of sale — we do not sell personal information, so this right does not apply
  • Non-discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

9.3 Rights Under Malaysia PDPA

Malaysian users have the right to:

  • Access — request access to your personal data held by us
  • Correction — request correction of personal data that is inaccurate, incomplete, misleading, or not up to date
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw consent for the processing of your personal data
  • Make complaints — lodge a complaint with the Department of Personal Data Protection

9.4 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at myprojectfit@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days (GDPR/PDPA) or 45 days (CCPA). We may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request.

You can also delete your account and associated data through the App's settings.
